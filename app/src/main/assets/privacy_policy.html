Privacy Policy — Sehat

Last updated: April 2026

Sehat (سیہت) is a personal health tracking app built on a single principle: your health data is yours alone.

What data Sehat collects

DataPurposeStorage
Step count, distance, calories, durationPedometerOn your device only
Heart rate (BPM) and activity contextHeart rate monitorOn your device only
Personal info (name, age, height, weight)BMI & calorie estimatesOn your device only
Custom meditation sound URIsMeditationPointer stored on device
App settings and preferencesConfigurationOn your device only

What Sehat does NOT do

  • Does not transmit any data to any server or third party
  • Does not request the INTERNET permission — the app cannot make network calls
  • Does not collect analytics or usage statistics
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone

Camera

The camera is used solely to measure heart rate by analyzing light reflected from your fingertip. No images or video are ever recorded, saved, or transmitted. The camera is active only while measuring heart rate.

Step sensor

The app uses the device's built-in step counter sensor via the ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION permission. Data is processed on-device; only the final step count and derived metrics are stored locally.

Network access

Sehat does not request the INTERNET permission. The app is technically incapable of making any network connection.

Data backup and export

The in-app backup feature lets you export your health data to a JSON file on your own device. You choose the location. The file is not transmitted anywhere — it stays on your device until you move or delete it.

Data deletion

To delete all data stored by Sehat, uninstall the app. Android will remove all app data automatically. If you exported a backup file, delete it manually.
